HR Document Expiration Tracking

What this system does: Automatically checks employee documents, finds what's missing or expiring, and sends a clear weekly report - without manual work.

The Problem

HR had to manually review large Excel files every week to check passports, medical certificates, and other mandatory documents.

This process was:

  • time-consuming
  • easy to get wrong
  • stressful close to deadlines
  • risky for compliance

Missing one document could stop operations.

The Solution

A fully automated system that:

  • reads the latest HR spreadsheet
  • checks all document expiration dates
  • Flags missing and expiring documents (30-day warning)
  • Applies rules based on employee role
  • Highlights expired items in red in Excel
  • Sends a structured email report automatically
Runs automatically on schedule. No manual effort required.
